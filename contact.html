Directions

From points West:

Mass Pike East Take exit 25 (South Boston/Summer Street) on the right. Keep left at fork and continue to Congress Street. Take left on Congress St. Take left on A Street. Blue Dragon will be on your right/at the corner of Melcher Street

From points North:

Take I-93 south (Boston). Take exit 23 (purchase street) on right. Continue onto John F Fitzgerald Surface Road (Purchase Street). Turn left onto Congress Street. Turn right on A Street. Blue Dragon will be on your right/at the corner of Melcher Street

From points South:

Take I-93 North. Take exit 20 (I-90, Logan Airport, Worcester, South Station) on right. Take Seaport Boulevard ramp on left. Turn sharply left onto Congress Street. Take left on A Street. Blue Dragon will be on your right/at the corner of Melcher Street

Parking

Validated Garage Parking is available.

In cooperation with the Necco Street In-door Garage, during designated times, Blue Dragon will offer guests a special $5 validated parking rate. The $5 special rate is only available after 5:00pm Monday - Friday or all day Saturday ONLY in the in-door Necco Street covered garage located at 10 Necco Street (the corner of A Street and Necco Street). This rate is NOT available for the nearby open air flat parking lot.

Entrance to Necco Street Garage is at 10 Necco Street, short 1-block walk to Blue Dragon. Must be in garage by 10:00pm (access to garage closes). Exit remains open since payment is automated via credit or debit card. Payment locations at garage are in elevator lobbies and exit gates.

NOTE: Evening guests: Be sure to bring your parking ticket with you. After 10:00pm, you'll need it for the swipe reader to open lobby doors. ~ Ask the host stand for validation card that you will use during payment process.
